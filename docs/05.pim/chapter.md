AtroPIM is an open-source product information management (PIM) system. This means it's a software tool that helps businesses organize and manage all their product information in one central location.

Main features are:

- Centralized data repository: AtroPIM eliminates the need for scattered spreadsheets or databases by providing a single place to store all your product information
- Customization: It offers high configurability, allowing you to tailor the system to fit your specific needs. This includes customizing data models and user interfaces.
- API-centric architecture: AtroPIM uses an API-first approach, which means everything is done through an API, making it easy to integrate with other systems.
- Open-source: One of the big advantages of AtroPIM is that it's open-source. This means it's free to use and modify, and there's a community of developers who contribute to its development.
