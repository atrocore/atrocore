Before diving into the demo, we recommend taking the time to [configure the system](02.configuring-atrocore) to suit your needs. Understanding key terms such as entities, attributes, and classifications will empower you to make the most of AtroPIM's capabilities.

While you're free to explore [our public demo](https://demo.atropim.com/) (login: admin, password: admin) as a reference and example, be aware that the public demo has scheduled logouts every two hours, and any actions you take during your session will not be saved.

Once you're comfortable with the basics, consider checking out our tutorials specifically tailored to [importing your first product list](03.first-steps-with-atrocore). These tutorials provide step-by-step guidance, ensuring a seamless onboarding experience.

We're excited to support you as you harness the AtroPIM tools to effectively manage your product data. Let's begin!
