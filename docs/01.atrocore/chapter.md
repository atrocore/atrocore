AtroCore software is an open-source data platform designed for businesses to:

- Manage data and business processes: Organize and store various types of data relevant to your business operations.
- Develop custom applications: Build web-based applications tailored to your specific needs, like systems for managing Master Data (MDM), Product Information (PIM), or Digital Assets (DAM).
- Connect with other systems: Integrate AtroCore with existing software you use to ensure seamless data flow and avoid manual data entry.
